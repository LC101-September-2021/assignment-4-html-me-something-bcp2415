Job History

Teacher

I stumbled into a job as a high school Latin teacher. It turned out to be fun.

After a few years I wasn't learning as much as I had at the beginning. I got restless and decided I needed a Ph.D. in Philosophy. It was obvious.

Philosopher

In graduate school, I learned to write my papers using . This was my first step into coding.

After finishing the Ph.D., I was hired as a post-doc in Oxford. This was a lot of fun: no end of challenge and learning. I published a few papers and gave a few talks.

Then it was time to re-job. For several reasons, I elected to return to teaching Latin.

Teacher

I found more to learn in the new Latin job, and wonderful colleagues. But my learning again declined over time, and I miss being challenged in my work.

Web Developer
